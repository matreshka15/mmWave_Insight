    参考资料¶

    本页收集了毫米波雷达学习和开发的相关资源，包括教材、论文、在线课程和开发工具。

    📚 经典教材¶

    雷达基础¶

    1. Skolnik, M. I. (2008). Radar Handbook (3rd ed.). McGraw-Hill.
    2. 雷达领域的经典参考书
    3. 涵盖雷达系统的各个方面

    4. 适合工程师和研究人员

    5. Richards, M. A. (2014). Fundamentals of Radar Signal Processing (2nd ed.). McGraw-Hill.

    6. 专注于信号处理
    7. 包含 MATLAB 示例代码

    8. 深入讲解 FFT、CFAR 等算法

    9. Mahafza, B. R. (2013). Radar Systems Analysis and Design Using MATLAB (3rd ed.). CRC Press.

    10. 结合理论和实践
    11. 丰富的 MATLAB 代码

    12. 适合自学

    13. 张光义，赵玉洁 (2017). 雷达原理. 国防工业出版社.

    14. 国内经典教材
    15. 系统完整
    16. 中文资料

    毫米波雷达¶

    1. Hasch, J., et al. (2012). "Millimeter-wave technology for automotive radar sensors in the 77 GHz frequency band." IEEE Transactions on Microwave Theory and Techniques, 60(3), 845-860.
    2. 汽车雷达技术综述
    3. 77 GHz 频段特性

    4. 实际应用案例

    5. Patole, S. M., et al. (2017). "Automotive radars: A review of signal processing techniques." IEEE Signal Processing Magazine, 34(2), 22-35.

    6. 信号处理技术综述
    7. FMCW 雷达详解
    8. 目标检测和跟踪

    阵列信号处理¶

    1. Van Trees, H. L. (2002). Optimum Array Processing. Wiley-Interscience.
    2. 阵列信号处理经典
    3. 波束形成理论

    4. DOA 估计算法

    5. Stoica, P., & Moses, R. (2005). Spectral Analysis of Signals. Pearson.

    6. 频谱估计理论
    7. 参数估计方法
    8. MUSIC、ESPRIT 算法

    📝 重要论文¶

    FMCW 雷达¶

    1. Rohling, H. (1983). "Radar CFAR Thresholding in Clutter and Multiple Target Situations." IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems.

    2. Stove, A. G. (1992). "Linear FMCW radar techniques." IEE Proceedings F-Radar and Signal Processing.

    3. Pfeffer, C., et al. (2013). "A FMCW MIMO Radar System for Frequency-Division Multiple TX-Beamforming." IEEE Transactions on Microwave Theory and Techniques.

    目标检测¶

    1. Finn, H. M., & Johnson, R. S. (1968). "Adaptive detection mode with threshold control as a function of spatially sampled clutter-level estimates." RCA Review.

    2. Gandhi, P. P., & Kassam, S. A. (1988). "Analysis of CFAR processors in nonhomogeneous background." IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems.

    微多普勒¶

    1. Chen, V. C. (2011). "The Micro-Doppler Effect in Radar." Artech House.

    2. Kim, Y., & Ling, H. (2009). "Human activity classification based on micro-Doppler signatures using a support vector machine." IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing.
